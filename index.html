About the Hotel

A sanctuary by the sea

Azure Sands is a boutique hotel crafted for travelers who value quiet elegance. Nestled steps from the shoreline, our property blends natural textures, soft light, and attentive service into an experience that feels both luxurious and effortless.

Each room is designed with rest in mind — crisp linens, curated local art, and panoramic views that invite you to slow down. Whether you are here for a weekend escape or an extended stay, we welcome you with warmth and professionalism.
